Principles

Altair Alpha
Altair Alpha is a business active in used books and paper recycling industry. As books are wonderful media to transfer knowledge and enlighten readers’ minds, Altair Alpha’s mission is to recirculate as much as it can the discarded books back into the hands of readers; either through various local market segments, donations, export, or online portals. Through its various trade and non-trade routes, the company believes it has been able to save the most discarded books that flow through its warehouse by percentage wise. Given books degrade cardboard output of recyclers as well as require specific technology to recycle them properly, Altair Alpha dedicatedly works with recyclers that have the technology to process recycling books and and export the outputs to Southeast Asia market. Thus, the company prevents the books to mindlessly transported into the landfill. Since its existence, Altair Alpha estimates to divert few millions books out of the landfill annually.

Altair Alpha’s Philosophy

  • Serve a Meaningful Mission – Altair Alpha believes in solving social problems to be the new essential building block of modern capitalism. We believe in doing good, behaving well, being positive, and creating social values in every business that we engage. That makes every morning exciting on our adventure of making the world a better place and makes our time meaningful. We are delighted by being a part of books and recycling paper industry where we can handle heavy logistic works for our literacy partners, give used books another opportunity from being transported to the landfill, recycle books and paper with proper technology, stabilize the recycling paper supply chain for industrial partners in Southeast Asia, and provide quality books-content to readers all over the world. It involves a little bit of dust on what we do everyday, but we are happy because we do a meaningful business.
  • Strive for Excellence – On execution, everything that is executed from Altair Alpha must be at highest dedication to quality.  We take pride in everything that has our name behind it.  Excellence does not naturally come out in business, gifted minds take countless of trials and errors, endure prolonged period of external and internal doubts, and go through major difficulties in attaining to their excellency.  We want to be the best of our capability in business, thus, we value dedication, continuously improving, open-minded, and, most of all, hard-work.
  • Deal Honorably – We have a very long term view on doing business.  Long term means life-time.  We want to do business with our suppliers, customers, and other stakeholders on life-time basis.  And if we are lucky enough to survive ups and downs of the market and have a second, third, or later generations that run our company – they will also do business on life-time view basis.  Thus toward each and every transaction, it is either a happy transaction or we will continue to work it out toward happiness and for the relationship in the long-run.
